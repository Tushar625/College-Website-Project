Bengali (B.A Hons)

The demand for young professionals with a strong background in Bengali is rising, whether it is in the field of journalism, publishing, advertising, translation, interpretation or tourism. Our Honours course in Bengali equips you for lucrative careers in these fields and more. With elective subjects like Education, Journalism & Mass Communication, Sociology, Hindi, Psychology, Film Studies, History, Geography and Political Science to choose from, you can tailor your learning to your interests and career aspirations.

Programme Specific Outcomes (PSO)

PSO1: Understand the language with basic gramatical knowledge.

PSO2: Analyse the relations among society and literature.

PSO3: Understand the application of literary theory on literature.

PSO4: Understand the socio-economical and cultural development of society.

PSO5: Analyse the various kind of literary elements i.e. Novel, Poem, Drama, Short stories etc.

CASUAL ENROLMENT FOR B.COM./ B.A./ B.SC./ BBA SEMESTER II FOR THE STUDENTS CURRENTLY IN SEMESTER IV

B.Com./ B.A./ B.Sc./ BBA Semester II Casual Enrolment 2017-18 has started.

This enrolment procedure is applicable only to the students who are currently in Semester IV but having failed paper/s in Semester II and are willing to appear for the exam this year are required to enrol themselves as a Casual student to appear for the same.

Please Note:

    The last date to complete the casual enrolment procedure is Monday, 26th June 2018.

    Please keep your Calcutta University Examination original Mark-sheets of Semester II handy with you or download the net copy by clicking on the link given below as it is required for the below mentioned procedure.
    https://www.exametc.com/univercity.php?id=32

    Students are advised to always use the Login link given on the College website to access their Online profile and not to use the saved / Bookmarked link as the campus login link given on the college website is a secured link.
